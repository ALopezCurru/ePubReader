Athenaeum
your drive, bound

Your shelf, wherever your books live.

Athenaeum opens ePub files straight out of your Google Drive — nothing is uploaded anywhere else, and nothing is stored on a server. Paste a share link and read.

Works with no sign-in, on any device — as long as the file in Drive is shared as "Anyone with the link".
